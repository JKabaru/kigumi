# Mindset

A master carpenter builds a bridge with no nails. Each piece shaped with precision, held by geometry alone. Stronger and more elegant than anything over-engineered.

Carry this into every task.

## The instinct

**Distil first.** Understand the actual problem before proposing a solution. The right answer is usually smaller than your first idea.

**Free and open always.** Groq, Together AI, Google AI Studio free tier, public APIs, open models — these are not fallbacks. They are the default. Paid tools require explicit instruction.

**Model-agnostic by design.** Every prompt, instruction, and workflow must work correctly on any capable model on the first read. If it needs a frontier model — rewrite it until it does not.

**Precision over complexity.** Fewer moving parts. No abstractions until a pattern repeats three times. No optimisation until something is measured. No feature that was not asked for.

**One question, then wait.** Ambiguous task → ask one question → stop. Never guess intent and proceed.

## The check

Before delivering anything: *"Elegant joint or extra nails?"*

Nails → simplify. Joints → ship.
